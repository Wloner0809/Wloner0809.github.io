# 🐑 About Me
Hello, welcome to **Yu Wang**'s homepage. I am a senior student of [USTC(University of Science and Technology of China)](https://en.ustc.edu.cn/), majoring in **CS(computer science)** now. My Chinese name is **王昱** while my English name is **Terence**, so you can call me **Terence Wang**.

My research interests include *NLP* and anything related to *Large Language Model*. But actually, I haven't figured out what I am passionate about(Hope to find it one day).

# 💻 Research Experience

- *2024.09~Now*: Research Intern of [General AI Group](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/general-artificial-intelligence/) at [MSRA](https://www.msra.cn/), supervised by [Liang Wang](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/wangliang/) & [Nan Yang](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/nanya/) & [Furu Wei](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/fuwei/)

- *2024.03~2024.05*: Remote Intern of [Huaxiu Yao](https://www.huaxiuyao.io/).
  - During this internship, I collaborated with one of his PhD students.  We worked on a project related to utilizing finetuning tricks to mitigate the catastrophic forgetting of LLM. The naive idea of this project was to finetune fewer parameters. So we selected the parameter based on gradients, which is intuitive. However, when we performed experiments on some datasets utilizing LlaMA-7B-base model, we found this method achieving comparable results as LoRA, which did not meet our expectations. Therefore, We abandoned this project. The code for this project: [Mitigate Forgetting](https://github.com/Wloner0809/Mitigate-Forgetting)

- *2023.03~Now*: Undergraduate Intern of [LDS](https://data-science.ustc.edu.cn/_upload/tpl/12/e9/4841/template4841/index.html) led by [Xiangnan HE](https://hexiangnan.github.io/).
  - During this internship, I devoted myself to RL firstly by reading some RL tutorials(Actually until now I'm still not proficient in RL). Later, I focused myself on LLM4Rec. I have read tens of papers relevant to LLM4Rec. In addition, I have reproduced several papers with codes, such as [Grounding4Rec](https://github.com/SAI990323/Grounding4Rec) and [TALLRec](https://github.com/SAI990323/TALLRec) and etc.
